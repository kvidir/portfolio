About Me

I'm Víðir Helgi, also known online as TheNotoriousMonsi, I'm the CEO and Founder of Agatech and creator of Lambro.

My entrepreneurial journey began at the age of 17, managing Airbnb listings and diving into the world of online business. That early experience sparked a deep interest in digital tools, marketing, and automation, eventually leading me into the world of cryptocurrency and blockchain.

Without any prior programming background, I taught myself how to write smart contracts and launched the Lambro token, which became the foundation for the Agatech ecosystem, a full-stack innovation in the Web3 space.

Today, I lead Agatech with a mission to merge technology and creativity to build decentralized tools for the future. This entire experience is what inspired me to pursue software development professionally and continue growing my skills every day.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio.
